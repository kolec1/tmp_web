FAQ

Common questions

Are there any setup or one-time fees? ▼
Yes — an initial implementation project covers environment setup, menu configuration, POS integration, and UI/UX customisation for kiosks or mobile apps. These are one-time costs quoted separately based on your specific POS system and branding requirements. Contact us and we'll prepare a full proposal including a project breakdown.
Can I add or remove modules later? ▼
Absolutely. Ordering Stack is designed to grow with you. You can add new modules or remove existing ones on any monthly billing cycle — no penalties, no lock-in.
How does kiosk pricing work exactly? ▼
The first kiosk at each restaurant location is billed at the full rate (€127 / 545 zł per month). Every additional kiosk at the same location is billed at a reduced rate (€39 / 165 zł per month). This lets you scale your kiosk coverage without a linear cost increase.
What POS systems does Ordering Stack integrate with? ▼
We have production integrations with Oracle Simphony, MICROS 3700, SICOM, Agnis, Adith X2, and Dotykacka. If your POS isn't listed, we can evaluate a custom integration — reach out to our sales team with details.
Are there any costs NOT included in the monthly fee? ▼
A few third-party pass-through costs sit outside the subscription: GIS/mapping API fees for address autocomplete in delivery ordering, payment gateway transaction fees, and SMS messaging costs. All of these are charged at cost — we don't mark them up.
Do you offer volume discounts for large chains? ▼
Yes. For chains with 15+ locations we negotiate custom pricing. Get in touch and we'll put together a tailored proposal.
